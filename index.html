Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between virgin and refined coconut oil?

At NutriMax, we offer virgin coconut oil, extracted through a cold-pressing method. This process preserves the oil's natural goodness, delicate aroma, and potent benefits. Refined coconut oil undergoes additional processing, resulting in a higher smoke point for cooking but with a milder flavor and aroma.

How can I use NutriMax Coconut Oil for my skin?

NutriMax Coconut Oil is a versatile moisturizer suitable for all skin types. You can use it directly on your face and body as a daily moisturizer or as a nourishing overnight treatment. It's also a popular choice for removing makeup!

Can I use NutriMax Coconut Oil for hair care?

Absolutely! Coconut oil's rich fatty acids can help strengthen hair strands, promote healthy growth, and combat dryness. Apply a moderate amount to your hair as a pre-shampoo treatment or leave-in conditioner for added shine and manageability.
